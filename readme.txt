== Description ==

**Kinescope** - is an ecosystem of products for business to handle online video on every step. From video player to infrastructure - professional video solutions are available for everyone.

Growing your business with the power of video becomes easier with the Kinescope WordPress plugin. It allows to embed video player in any spot of your website just pasting video link from Kinescopeâ€™s dashboard.

Step-by-step guide to use the plugin:

1. Register your account at Kinescope here (https://app.kinescope.io/user/create) (you can always start for Free);
2. Upload your first video in the Kinescope's dashboard (or run a live stream) 
3. Copy video link (by right clocking on the video title in the catalog or from video settings page);
4. Paste video link in your WordPress visual editor and .. hooray - your player is in place!

Don't forget to make the most out of your content by customising your player template, adding chapters, subtitles and even CTA buttons. Learn more at kinescope.io (https://kinescope.io/)

[ ! Notice ] Protecting your videos from unauthrized download (DRM encryption) requires your website to have an active SSL certificate. 

How else you can benefit from Kinescope:

- Multiformat uploading
- Redundant  storage
- Fast and reliable video conversion up to 4K
- Live streaming
- Modern customisable video player
- Video ads support (VAST, VPAID, IMA)
- SEO optimised player
- Powerful API and SDK to build on top of the platform

Unlike others, Kinescope is building its own high-performance ready metal infrastructure and networks. Operating globally as a CDN it offers fair and affordable pay-as-you-go pricing for businesses of any size. Mail us at hello@kinescope.io for collaboration requests.

== Installation ==

1. Upload plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or install it from 'Add New' on 'Plugins' page.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
